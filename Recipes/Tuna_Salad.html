Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 ca(142g) of tuna, drained
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise (or Greek yogurt for a lighter option)
  • 1 stalk of celery, finely chopped (optional, but adds great crunch)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Drain the Tuna: Open the can of tuna and thoroughly drain out the water or oil. Place the tuna in a small bowl.
  2. Mix: Add the mayonnaise (or yogurt) and the chopped celery (if using) to the bowl with the tuna.
  3. Season: Add a generous pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Combine: Mix all the ingredients well with a fork until they are fully combined and have a creamy, slightly chunky texture.
  5. Serve: Use immediately to make a sandwich, scoop onto crackers, or serve over a bed of fresh lettuce.
