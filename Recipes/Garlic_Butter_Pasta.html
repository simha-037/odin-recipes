Garlic Butter Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound (about 225g) of spaghetti or linguine
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of reserved pasta water
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish, optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil the pasta according to the package directions in salted water. Before draining, scoop out about a cup of the starchy cooking water and set it aside. Drain the rest of the pasta.
  2. Make the Sauce: In the same pot or a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-low heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant (don't let the garlic burn!).
  3. Combine: Add the drained pasta to the pot with the garlic butter. Toss to coat.
  4. Emulsify: Add about $1/4$ cup of the reserved pasta water to the pot. Stir or toss vigorously; the starch in the water will mix with the butter to create a light, creamy sauce. Add more pasta water a tablespoon at a time if the sauce is too thick.
  5. Finish: Stir in the Parmesan cheese (if using) and season with salt and pepper. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
